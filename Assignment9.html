Titanic (1997)

Director: James Cameron Genre: Romance, Drama, Disaster Starring: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet, Billy Zane Titanic is an iconic and epic romance-drama film that captured the hearts of audiences worldwide. Directed by the masterful James Cameron, this critically acclaimed and record-breaking film tells the story of two young individuals from different social classes who fall deeply in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

Harry Potter

The Harry Potter film series is based on the globally popular book series written by J.K. Rowling. It consists of eight movies that were released between 2001 and 2011, and they collectively tell the story of young wizard Harry Potter as he battles against the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. The movies were produced by Warner Bros. Pictures and spanned a decade of filmmaking. They are known for their exceptional visual effects, intricate set designs, and talented ensemble cast.

GOLDEN EYE

"GoldenEye" is the seventeenth installment in the iconic James Bond film series. It was released in 1995 and marks the debut of Pierce Brosnan as the suave British secret agent, James Bond. Here's some information about the film: - Plot: After a six-year hiatus, Bond is reactivated to investigate the theft of the control disk for the GoldenEye satellite, which has the power to devastate electronic infrastructure. Bond must stop the villainous former MI6 agent, Alec Trevelyan, known as Agent 006, who plans to use GoldenEye to wreak havoc on the world's financial markets.
